Five Can Soup

Delicious and quick mail recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can prepared chili with beans
  • 1 can whole kernel corn
  • 1 can vegetable beef soup
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers

Directions

  1. Stir chili, corn, vegetable beef soup, tomato soup, and diced tomatoes with green chile peppers together in a saucepan over medium-high heat; cook until hot, 5 to 10 minutes.
