Easy Vegan Black Bean Soup

Delicous vegan meal soup recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1 large carrot, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 cans black beans, drained
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon cumin

Directions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a stockpot over medium-high heat; saute onion, green bell pepper, celery, carrot, and garlic until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Add black beans, diced tomatoes, water, and cumin to onion mixture; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until flavors have combined, at least 20 minutes.
